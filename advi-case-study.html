Overview

A healthcare digital presence that needed to match the complexity of its work.

ADVI Health had a lot going on. Multiple service areas, complex user journeys, and a site that wasn't connecting it all clearly. They needed a proper redesign that elevated how they came across and made things easier to navigate.

This project is the best example of my AI-augmented workflow running end-to-end. AI helped the research and audit, Relume built the structure, and I took ux ownership in Figma.

Process

The full AI-augmented workflow.

Step 1

AI Research & Audit

I kicked off with an AI-assisted audit of the existing site. Finding the friction, the gaps, and where users were likely getting lost. The output shaped the entire project direction.

Step 2

Stakeholder Meetings

I ran sessions with the ADVI team to get a proper understanding of their audience, their goals, and the complexity of what the site needed to do. Healthcare UX needs to be precise.

Step 3 — 27h Saved

Sitemap & Wireframes in Relume

I built the full sitemap and wireframes in Relume, then exported them straight into Figma. This one phase saved roughly 27 hours compared to doing it the traditional way.

Step 4

Design & Interaction in Figma

Once the structure was solid, this is where I stepped in fully. Visual language, design system, interactions, all designed in Figma. The AI did the groundwork, but the design is mine.

Step 5

Design System Handoff

I prepared the full design system and handed it over to the dev team.. Clean handoffs make for clean builds.
