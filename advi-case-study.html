[ Overview ]

A healthcare digital presence that needed to match the complexity of its work.

ADVI Health had a lot going on — multiple service areas, complex user journeys, and a site that wasn't connecting it all clearly. They needed a proper redesign that elevated how they came across and made things genuinely easier to navigate.

This is my most complete showcase of the AI-augmented workflow end-to-end. AI handled the research and audit, Relume built the structure, and I took full creative ownership in Figma — designing every component, variable, and interaction by hand.

[ Process ]

The full AI-augmented workflow.

Step 1

AI Research & Audit

I kicked off with an AI-assisted audit of the existing site — finding friction, gaps, and where users were likely getting lost. The output shaped the entire project direction.

Step 2

Stakeholder Meetings

I ran sessions with the ADVI team to properly understand their audience, their goals, and the complexity the site needed to handle. Healthcare UX needs to be precise.

Step 3 — 27h saved

Sitemap & Wireframes in Relume

I built the full sitemap and all wireframes in Relume, then exported them straight into Figma. This phase alone saved roughly 27 hours versus doing it manually.

Step 4

Design & Interaction in Figma

With the structure solid, I took full creative ownership in Figma — visual language, design system, variables, and every interaction. The AI did the groundwork. The design is mine.

Step 5

Design System Handoff

A complete design system — components, tokens, spacing, type — packaged up and handed to the development team. Clean handoffs make for clean builds.
