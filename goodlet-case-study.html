[ Overview ]

The UK rental market needed a better way.

Tenant problem

Good tenants lose out to richer applicants all the time. There's no way to show a landlord that you've always looked after your homes — no track record, no verified history, nothing to go on except a bank statement.

Landlord problem

Landlords do their best with basic reference checks and a gut feeling. But there's no real way to know if someone will be a good fit before both sides have already invested time and energy.

[ What was designed ]

Six core features.

Tenant

Home Story

A verified visual portfolio of homes cared for — photos, improvements, and landlord-verified condition reports that travel with you permanently.

Tenant

Care Score

A dynamic score built from verified references and tenancy history. Grows with every tenancy — and can replace guarantor requirements.

Both

Compatibility Matching

Matching on lifestyle, lease preferences, and care signals — never on demographics. Protected characteristics stay private by design.

Tenant

One-Tap Apply

Home Story pre-fills every application. Apply in seconds with a verified profile already attached — no 40-field forms.

Landlord

Tenant Scouting

Browse live tenant profiles before listing — find the right fit proactively before a vacancy even opens.

Both

Protected by Design

WCAG AA, keyboard navigation, skip links, ARIA labels, and reduced motion support — accessibility baked in from day one.

[ How it was built ]

Designed with Claude AI.
