Overview

A gap in the UK rental market

Tenant problem

Never judged on care

Good tenants lose out to richer applicants all the time. There's no way to show a landlord that you've always looked after your homes. No track record, no verified history, nothing to go on except a bank statement and a legal due-dilligence.

Landlord problem

Guesswork screening

Landlords do their best with basic reference checks and a gut feeling. But there's no real way to know if someone will be a good fit for looking after your asset before both sides have already invested time and energy.

What was designed

Six core features.

Tenant

Home Story

A verified visual portfolio of homes cared for. Photos, improvements, and landlord-verified condition reports that travel with you permanently.

Tenant

Care Score

A dynamic score built from verified references and tenancy history. Grows with every tenancy, and can replace guarantor requirements.

Both

Compatibility Matching

Matching on lifestyle, lease preferences, and care signals, never on demographics. Protected characteristics stay private by design.

Tenant

One-Tap Apply

Home Story pre-fills every application. Apply in seconds with a verified profile already attached. No 40-field forms.

Landlord

Tenant Scouting

Browse live tenant profiles before listing. Find the right fit proactively before a vacancy even opens.

Both

Protected by Design

WCAG AA, keyboard navigation, skip links, ARIA labels, and reduced motion support. Accessibility is the most important.
